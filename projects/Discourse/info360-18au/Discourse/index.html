Political Discussions Made Easy

Overview

Problem:
According to a national study of student voters conducted across more than 1,100 college campuses in the United States, only about one third of students registered to vote in 2014 actually voted. This low voter turnout for young people is not just a problem in the United States, it is also a problem in other developed countries such as the United Kingdom. Young people often feels disconnected from politics because young people are less likely to be targeted by campaigns, and thus rarely hear about the relevant issues that concern them.

Solution:
Understanding the need to make content more digestible and streamlined to appeal to the youth, we have modeled our solution to resemble a highly popular, fast-paced app such as Tinder.  By creating a simple, familiar, yet highly effective application, Discourse will match young voters with the candidates who share their beliefs, morals, and values to help them make educated decisions and carry out their civic duty

How it Works

Swipe

Swipe right on issues you agree with and swipe left on issues you disagree with. It's that simple.

Whether you are leaning left, right or anything in between, our unbiased algorithm will match you with the candidate you identify with.

Explore Your Political Identity

Whether it is understanding the issues that you and your candidate disagree with, or understanding where you lie on a political map, we provide the platform for you to understand your political identity.
