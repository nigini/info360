    Enhance the learning experience

    The United States holds about 1.1 million undergraduate and graduate international students in college institutions across the nation, out of 4.8 million international students worldwide.

    ​

    These International students will enter institutions that are predominantly if not exclusively English-speaking. And Although every student starting college will face many new challenges, such as higher expectations and busy schedules, international students face the additional obstacle of pursuing higher education in a language that is not their own. And overcoming the initial language barriers and cultural differences can be a big source of stress and anxiety for these students.

    #ANNOTIA

    A scannable solution to enhance student learning in higher education

    OUR SERVICE

    Annotia, a scannable solution. Our application is designed to help international students understand complex course information, in both exam and homework settings. The Annotia software will allow users to use their phone to scan unknown/unfamiliar vocabulary that is on their exams, and then will generate a rephrased or more coherent response that will give them context and insight into understanding the content.

      How it works  

    Step 1: Our login screen is simple and has two fields where the user can input their information, a forgot password link, and a sign-in button. The first field accepts the user’s UW netid and the second accepts a password. When the user clicks “Sign In” their input is checked against existing UW netid Databases for authentication.

    Step 2: Home Screen! We wanted our Home screen to have two simple options: Exam and Homework. Clicking “Exam” takes you to a code authentication screen, and clicking “Homework” takes you to the homework homepage where you can start using the scanner to find solutions!

    Step 3: Snapshot & Solutions! After the user has selected the assignment that they want assistance with (homework or exam), the application will prompt the user to focus their phone camera* on the assignment with the question/sentence/paragraph that they would like help with fixed within the box corners. Once they have what they want in focus, they tap the circular button below to take a picture to be analyzed for a richer context.
