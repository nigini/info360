A SOLUTION THAT ENHANCES COLLEGE READINESS TO HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS.

The Problem:

    The minimum requirement for most jobs today is a bachelor's degree. Many students of color face a myriad of barriers in gaining access to resources to help them enroll in college. The programs have a specific defined objective and fail to address the other obstacles that low-income students face.

The Solution:

    collegeSource presents an overview of the college application process in a chronological way through a timeline that is accessible from the home page and the headers. collegeSource is accessible to students from any place that has working wifi and a computer whether it be at home, on their phones, at school, in public libraries, or any other area. There are no downloads or accounts required to access the site, allowing for easy access.

Our Prototype

Home Page

This is the first page of collegeSource. Here, users can determine their class status, as well as buttons to login or sign-up!

Junior Year Page

Users can click for resources that revolve junior class standing while preparing for college.

SATs and ACTs

This page provides a general overview of both exams, providing FAQs and definitions of examination terms.

Login Page

Users will be prompted from the home page to login to their collegeSource account.

Profile Page

Users can sign up for a collegeSource account while having the ability to edit their account information when needed.

Landing Page

This page includes a To-Do list of college-prep tasks for each user to view and complete based on their information from profile page.
